pd.cut() :将某一列按照几个标准分类

housing["income_cat"] = pd.cut(housing["median_income"],
                                   bins=[0., 1.5, 3.0, 4.5, 6., np.inf],
labels=[1, 2, 3, 4, 5])

category 1 ranges from 0 to 1.5 (i.e., less than $15,000), category 2 from 1.5 to 3, and so on

pd.DataFrame.drop(): 删除某一行或某一列

pandas.DataFrame.drop(labels=None, 
                      axis=0,
                     index=None,
                     columns=None,
                     level=None,
                     inplace=False,
                     errors='raise')

-- labels 单个标签或者标签列表
-- axis=0 默认 删除index
-- axis=1 指定删除列
-- inplace=True 修改原数据, 否则会创造copy
-- level 针对多重索引 指定级别
-- index 指定索引
-- columns 指定列名
